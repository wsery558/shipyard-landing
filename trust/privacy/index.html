Privacy

Effective date: 2026-01-24.

What we collect

  • Waitlist submissions (email, persona selection, optional receipt email).
  • Site interactions for analytics (page path, timestamp, language, clicks).
  • Lightweight metadata when you visit via UTM or referrer.

We keep records only as long as needed to support your waitlist request.

What we avoid

  • We do not capture secrets, API keys, or private source code through this site.
  • Product data remains local to your run and is not sent to this landing page.
  • Embedded Google Forms only store what you submit within the form.

Telemetry & third parties

Cloudflare Pages records standard request logs, and form hosts retain submissions. We do not add extra monitoring beyond the minimal usage events listed above.

Questions? Email via the contact page or read security.txt for the disclosure channel.

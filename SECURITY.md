# Security Policy

Thanks for helping keep Shipyard safe.

## Reporting a vulnerability

Please report security issues privately.

- Email: wsery558@gmail.com
- Include: affected component, steps to reproduce, impact, and any safe proof-of-concept.

We try to acknowledge reports and coordinate a fix. We do not guarantee response or resolution timelines.

## Scope

This policy covers:
- The Shipyard landing site and public demo pages
- Shipyard-related repositories and distributed artifacts

## Safe harbor

Good-faith testing is welcome when it avoids harm:
- No data destruction
- No privacy invasion
- No service disruption
